Food Expiry

Welcome To Food Expiry

This app allows you to scan barcodes of disposable or non-disopable food items to keep your food fresh. Food Expiry will send you a push notification before your food expires. This prevents food waste and reminds you to make use of your food in your next meal.

APP FEATURES

Barcode Scanner

Use the barcode scanner to automatically retrieve the name and picture of your food item.

Pantry

Organize your food items in ascending order of the expiration date.

Expired Page

Keeps all of your expired items up to 7 days. You also have the option to move them to your grocery list.

Grocery List

Create a shopping list from your expired items or manually add more, so you can have a reference when you go to the store.
